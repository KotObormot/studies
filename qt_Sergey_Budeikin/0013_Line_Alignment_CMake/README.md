In this video tutorial, we'll look at how to align strings in Qt.
There are two methods for aligning strings: leftJustified() and rightJustified(). Each method aligns from right to left or vice versa. The video tutorial covers the rightJustified() method. The leftJustified() method works similarly to the right-aligned method, but aligns the string to the left.
